The Grow 2 (Chinese: 金箍棒传奇2：沙僧的逆袭) is a 2015 Chinese animated adventure comedy film directed by Ha Lei. It was released in China on May 29, 2015. The film was preceded by The Grow (2012).


== Voice cast ==
Yu Li
Wu Tian Hao
Rong Rong
Zhang Yuan


== Reception ==
By June 2, 2015, the film had earned CN¥4.36 million at the Chinese box office.
