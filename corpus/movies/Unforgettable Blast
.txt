Unforgettable Blast (Chinese: 迷与狂) is a 2015 Chinese comedy film directed by Yan Tinglu. It was released in China on May 22, 2015.


== Cast ==
Jiang Chao
Jiang Xueming
Wang Xia
Yang Qing
Luo Jia
Jiang Yihong
Lei Di


== Reception ==
By May 23, 2015, the film had earned CN¥0.71 million at the Chinese box office.
