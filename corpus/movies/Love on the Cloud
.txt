Love on the Cloud (Chinese: 微爱之渐入佳境) is a 2014 Chinese romantic comedy film directed by Gu Changwei. It was released on December 24.

== Reception ==
The film earned CN¥285.051 million at the Chinese box office.Derek Elley of Film Business Asia gave the film a 7 out of 10, saying that a "likeable cast gives an average rom-com/movie satire an unpretentious, fluffy charm."
