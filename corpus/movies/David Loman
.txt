David Loman (大尾鱸鰻) is a 2013 Taiwanese comedy film directed by Chiu Li-kwan.


== Cast ==
Chu Ke-liang
Amber Kuo
Tony Yang
Lin Mei-hsiu
Miao Ke-li as Nana


== Reception ==
It was the 3rd highest-grossing film of 2013 in Taiwan, with NT$428 million.Film Business Asia's Derek Elley gave the film a rating of 6 out of 10.A sequel, David Loman 2, was released on February 5, 2016. Aboriginals in Taiwan accused the film of bigotry.
