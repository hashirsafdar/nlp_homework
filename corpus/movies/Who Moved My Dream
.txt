Who Moved My Dream (Chinese: 谁动了我的梦想) is a 2014 Chinese comedy drama romance film directed by Wang Wei and Jackson Pat. It was released on December 11 in China.

== Box office ==
By December 12, 2014, the film had earned ¥0.72 million at the Chinese box office.
