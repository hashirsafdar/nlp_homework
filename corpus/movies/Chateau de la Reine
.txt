Château de la Reine (王妃の館, Ōhi no yakata) is a 2015 Japanese comedy drama film directed by Hajime Hashimoto. It was released on April 25, 2015.


== Cast ==
Yutaka Mizutani
Rena Tanaka
Kazue Fukiishi
Hiroyuki Onoue
Munetaka Aoki
Tomoya Nakamura
Yumi Adachi


== Reception ==
The film earned ¥50.51 million on its opening weekend in Japan.
