Freestyle MetalX is a sports video game created by British developer Deibus Studios and released in 2003 by Midway Games.The game's soundtrack to the game consists of hard rock and metal music, including bands such as Megadeth, Mötley Crüe, Motörhead, and Grade 8.


== Gameplay ==
Freestyle MetalX is a 3D motorcycle stunt game, that rewards the player for pulling off difficult moves.


== Reception ==

The game received "average" reviews on all platforms according to the review aggregation website Metacritic.
